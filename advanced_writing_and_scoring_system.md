# Advanced Writing, Coding & Scoring System

## 1. Content Scoring & Quality Assurance
*   **Evaluation Functions:** Heuristic and semantic evaluation of all generated text, messages, code, and scripts.
*   **Likelihood & Filtering:** Buffering and filtering outputs based on probability scores and quality thresholds.
*   **Data Quality:** Scoring the quality of training data (images, video, audio, text, code) and determining the percentage trained and passes needed.

## 2. Code & Script Debugging
*   **Syntax & Error Fixing:** Automated detection and correction of bugs, syntax errors, and logic flaws.
*   **Traceback Prevention:** Pre-execution analysis to prevent runtime tracebacks and crashes.
*   **Enhancement:** Suggesting improvements for code efficiency, readability, and architecture.

## 3. Creative Writing & Storyboard Engine
*   **Narrative Control:** Advanced tips, guides, and settings for style, themes, tone, details, and descriptors.
*   **Storyline Analysis:** Storyboard creation, plot consistency checking, and chronological timeline management.
*   **Grammar & Syntax Mastery:** Deep training on sentence structure, flow, nouns, verbs, past/present tense, conjunctions, adverbs, adjectives, prefixes, suffixes, and pronouns.
*   **Punctuation & Formatting:** Precise control over commas, periods, question marks, exclamation marks, quotation marks, brackets, parentheses, and fonts.

## 4. Sequential Generation & Word Chaining
*   **Possibility Mapping:** Generating all word combinations and possibilities for spelling and writing sentences.
*   **Contextual Adaptation:** Learning when and where to use specific vocabulary, organizing information, sorting data, and editing prompts dynamically to fit the persona and user intent.
